## Mobile Responsive Pattern:

### Text Sizes:
- Headings: `text-xl sm:text-2xl lg:text-3xl`
- Subheadings: `text-base sm:text-lg lg:text-xl`
- Body: `text-sm sm:text-base`
- Small text: `text-xs sm:text-sm`

### Spacing:
- Padding: `p-3 sm:p-4 lg:p-6`
- Gaps: `gap-2 sm:gap-3 lg:gap-4`
- Margins: `mb-2 sm:mb-3 lg:mb-4`

### Icons:
- Small: `w-4 h-4 sm:w-5 sm:h-5`
- Medium: `w-5 h-5 sm:w-6 sm:h-6 lg:w-7 lg:h-7`
- Large: `w-6 h-6 sm:w-8 sm:h-8 lg:w-10 lg:h-10`

### Grids:
- 1 column mobile, 2 tablet, 4 desktop: `grid-cols-1 sm:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-4`
- 1 column mobile, 2 desktop: `grid-cols-1 lg:grid-cols-2`

### Buttons:
- Padding: `px-3 sm:px-4 py-2 sm:py-2.5`
- Text: `text-sm sm:text-base`

### Cards:
- Padding: `p-3 sm:p-4 lg:p-5`
- Rounded: `rounded-lg sm:rounded-xl lg:rounded-2xl`

## Implementation Strategy:
Apply these patterns consistently across all pages for uniform mobile experience.
